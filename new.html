About Promises Of Restoration

Promises Of Restoration (P.O.R) is a premier non-profit facility located in Kirigiti, Kiambu. Our foundation is strictly Christian-based, dedicated to improving the physical, spiritual, and emotional health of those suffering from addiction. Guided by Integrity and Dedication, we offer safe and comprehensive counselling services to families and individuals alike.

Our Vision

To free affected individuals and their families from the chains of alcohol and drug addiction.

Our Mission

To improve the physical, spiritual, and emotional health of those suffering from addiction. We provide safe, transformative, and cost-effective substance abuse treatment and counselling services.
